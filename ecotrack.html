EcoTrack Mobile App

EcoTrack is a mobile application designed to empower individuals to live more sustainably by monitoring their daily environmental impact.

Features

  • Daily habit tracking to identify areas for environmental improvement.
  • Personalized analytics showing your carbon footprint reduction over time.
  • Daily eco-friendly tips and challenges to encourage sustainable living.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • Mobile-first Design Principles

Overview

The goal of EcoTrack is to make sustainability accessible and engaging. By providing users with clear data on their consumption habits, the app helps bridge the gap between environmental awareness and actionable change, fostering a community of eco-conscious individuals.
